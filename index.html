About Lindsay

I write adeptly for professional audiences and have organized high volumes of complex information for presentation. One of my fiercest passions continues to be in building and strengthening relationships through communication that fosters cohesive teamwork, understanding, and empowerment.


When not coding, I'm finding other ways to create. Favorites include: embroidering, sketching, and writing plot lines and mechanics for table top games.
